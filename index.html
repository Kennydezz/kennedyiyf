Our Story

SQS, also known as the Superb Quality Services, is a dynamic dance crew that originated in the heart of Mukuru Kayaba, Nairobi. Formed in 2010 by a group of passionate friends, SQS quickly gained recognition for their electrifying performances and unique style that blends hip-hop, contemporary, and street dance. What started as casual neighborhood dance battles soon turned into a serious pursuit of excellence, with the crew participating in and winning several local and national dance competitions.

The passion and dedication of its members, coupled with their tight-knit bond, have propelled SQS to become a symbol of creativity and resilience. Through their performances, SQS not only showcases their incredible talent but also spreads a message of unity and empowerment, inspiring young dancers across the city to follow their dreams.
